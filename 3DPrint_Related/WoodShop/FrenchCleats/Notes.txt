

To get 2 exact halves when cutting a 45 degree cleat angle

  For a 3" wide, 3/4" this piece
 1. Find width of piece divide by 2
	3/2 = 1-1/2

 2. Find 1/2 of width of piece
	3/4"/2 = 3/8"

 3. Finally width from blade to fence
	1-1/2 - 3/8
	1-4/8 - 3/8 == 1-1/8 fr0m blade to fence

 4. Cut 1/8" off sharp point edge

Binding blade
	Dull blade
	Fence not parallel to blade
	Better blade: 24 -30 for TPI for ripping, high count for cross cut
 	Combination:  40 - 50 TPI
	Cross Cut:	60 - 80
	Thin kerf .118 or less for 120 v saw
